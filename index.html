About Us


InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded in 2020. The organization works across India to create positive social impact through education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, community support, and skill development.

Our Ongoing Projects


Project Bachpanshala

Providing educational support and learning opportunities for underprivileged children.

Project Seva

Distributing food, clothes, and essential resources to people in need.

Project Jeev

Supporting stray animals through feeding, care, and welfare initiatives.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence.

Project Prakriti

Promoting environmental conservation through plantation and sustainability drives.

Project Vikas

Providing internships and skill-development opportunities for youth.

Social Impact


Through its initiatives, InAmigos Foundation has supported thousands of beneficiaries by providing food, education, environmental awareness, women empowerment programs, animal welfare services, and career development opportunities. The foundation believes that collective action can build a more inclusive and sustainable society.
